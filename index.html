WHO AM I?

I’m a woman in my early 30's who has always had a deep connection with nature and the outdoors.
Some of my earliest and happiest memories are of being outside,
soaking in the fresh air and freedom that only the natural world can offer.
While life took me in different directions for a while, the last six years have brought me back to where my heart belongs —
exploring the outdoors, especially hiking in the mountains and camping under the stars.

My grandfather has called me “Jullan” for as long as I can remember,
and that nickname feels perfectly tied to my identity as an adventurer and lover of the outdoors.
These days, I spend as much time as I can exploring the incredible Nordic landscapes.
From the rugged mountain trails to the quiet beauty of a forest at dusk,
there’s nothing that compares to the serenity and inspiration I find in nature.

Sleeping in the mountains has become one of my greatest joys.
It’s where I sleep the best, surrounded by the stillness of the wilderness and the comforting sounds of nature.
Whether it’s a short day hike to a stunning viewpoint or a multi-day exploration deep into remote terrain,
I thrive on the opportunity to disconnect from the everyday and connect with the beauty of the world around me.

Let’s explore, learn, and preserve our natural world together!
